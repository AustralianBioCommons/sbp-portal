Structure Prediction

AlphaFold2 brought about a revolution in structural biology by enabling high quality predictions of protein structure using evolutionary information extracted from multiple sequence alignments. Predicted structures can be used to inform downstream biological hypotheses by providing a direct representation of functional sites as well as uncovering homologous relationships with highly divergent ancestors. AI models have since been extended to support diverse molecular classes including nucleotides, small-molecules and post-translational modifications as well as providing the ability to apply implicit prediction constraints.
